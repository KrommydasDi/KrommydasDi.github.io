I am a theoretical physicist employing differential geometry, topology, and continuum field theories to study biological and bio-inspired physical systems. Currently, my research focuses on the emergence and functionality of order in living matter, as well as shape and pattern formation during development.

A parallel aspect of my work is to generalize theorems of General Relativity, such as the Penrose–Hawking singularity theorems, to semiclassical and quantum gravity. This includes constraining exotic spacetimes (e.g., bouncing cosmologies and traversable wormholes) by applying basic requirements that physical matter must satisfy, such as energy conditions.
